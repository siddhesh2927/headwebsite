Premium Sound Experience

The SoundWave Pro X headphones deliver an exceptional audio experience with custom-tuned 40mm dynamic drivers that produce rich, detailed sound across all frequencies. The advanced active noise cancellation technology effectively blocks out ambient noise, allowing you to immerse yourself fully in your music.

Comfort and Durability

Designed for extended listening sessions, the Pro X features memory foam ear cushions covered in premium protein leather that conforms to your ears for a comfortable fit. The adjustable headband and swiveling ear cups ensure a perfect fit for any head size.

Wireless Freedom

With Bluetooth 5.0 technology, enjoy stable wireless connectivity with a range of up to 30 feet. The built-in battery provides up to 30 hours of playback time on a single charge, and the quick-charge feature gives you 5 hours of listening time with just 15 minutes of charging.

Intuitive Controls

The touch-sensitive controls on the ear cup allow you to adjust volume, skip tracks, and answer calls with simple gestures. The built-in microphone array ensures clear voice pickup for calls and voice assistants.
